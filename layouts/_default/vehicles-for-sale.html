Should I Buy a Clean Or Bebuilt Title Vehicle?

Rebuilt Title Vehicles

Rebuilt title vehicles can be a good investment. Many times you can save from $5000 to $15000 by buying a rebuilt title vehicle. It's not the best option for everyone though. First of all, do your research on the vehicle you're considering. Ask for damage pictures, and find out what caused the rebuilt title. Look it over and ask the seller to point out what has been done. Finally, rebuilt title vehicles can offer the most savings if you plan on keeping it for a while.

Clean Title Vehicles

Clean title vehicles are usually the safest investment. You'll pay more upfront, but you can be assured that it shouldn't have any more issues than any vehicle. You will want to check the vehicle history report to make sure that there have been no major accidents, even with a clean title. If you want us to research the history on a vehicle you're looking at, just contact us with the VIN, and we will see what we can find.
